The Call

Horror/Thriller

"The Call" is a South Korean thriller that revolves around two women, Seo-yeon and Young-sook, who are connected across time through a mysterious phone call. Seo-yeon, living in the present day, receives a phone call from Young-sook, who is living in the past, twenty years prior. As they converse, they realize they are neighbors living in the same house but in different time periods. However, their seemingly innocent communication takes a dark turn when Seo-yeon inadvertently alters Young-sook's past, leading to unforeseen consequences in the present. The movie escalates into a suspenseful battle between the two women as they try to change their destinies and rewrite history. With its innovative premise, gripping storyline, and twists, "The Call" keeps viewers captivated until its thrilling conclusion.
